Frequently Asked Questions

          

            

              
              
Can I generate my own animations?

              

                Yes. Upload a local video in the editor, tweak settings, and export instantly.
              

            

            

              
              
What are the quality levels?

              

                Low (80 cols), Medium (160 cols), and High (240 cols). Choose based on file size and sharpness.
              

            

            

              
              
Does it support reduced motion?

              

                The runtime can support reduced motion, but this build currently plays embeds by default.
